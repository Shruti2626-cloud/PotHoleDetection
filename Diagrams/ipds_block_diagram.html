Sensor Fusion Formula
severity = 0.7 × confidence + 0.3 × jerk_norm²
jerk_norm = clamp( (jerk − J_MIN) / (J_MAX − J_MIN), 0, 1 )
J_MIN = 0.0 m/s³  |  J_MAX = 20.0 m/s³
